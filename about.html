About This Website

"What's For Dinner?" is a fun and simple way to help you decide what to eat. Instead of stressing over meal choices, you can spin the wheels and let the website choose for you!

How It Works

  • First, spin the category wheel (like Fruits, Seafood, or Grains).
  • Then, spin the second wheel to get a specific food from that category.
  • Try something new or rediscover your favorites!

Our Goal

This website is designed to make meal planning easier, more fun, and a little more adventurous. It encourages healthy variety and helps users break out of repetitive eating habits.
